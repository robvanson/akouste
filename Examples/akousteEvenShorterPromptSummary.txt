✅ akoúste Markdown Cheat Sheet

Purpose: Listening experiments using CommonMark + extensions for stimuli & responses.
Do NOT add extra comments or hints.

Structure:

Cover Page:
[//coverpage]: # "Start of coverpage" … [//coverpage]: # "End of coverpage"
Experiment Page:
Include ## {{{!answerstogo!|font-weight:bold}}} answers to go
Settings Block:
[//label]: # "key:value"
Stimulus Table Block:
[//comment]: # "These are stimuli for this experiment"
[//stimulusX]: # "values"

Special Constructs:

Audio: [[[Button Text | ColumnName{style}]]]
Video: [[§ alt-text | ColumnName{width:30%;height:auto;} §]]
Image: {{{§ ColumnName | style §}}}
Text: {{{ColumnName | style }}}
Responses:

Likert/Radio: (())
VAS slider: >>----------<< (inside table)
Text input: <<| text {style} |>>
Keystroke: @@@ text [keys] {style} @@@



Settings Example:
[//parameter]: # "body.style: font-family: Arial;background-color:GhostWhite;"
[//parameter]: # "addDigest:false"

Key Rules:

Preserve HTML & JavaScript.
Keep empty lines around headers, tables, and paragraph-level HTML tags.
Preserve >>, <<, <<|, and |>> character combinations as-is.
Follow strict formatting for cover page, settings, and stimulus table.
